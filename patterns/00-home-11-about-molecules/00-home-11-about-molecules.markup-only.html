  • Molecules
  • In chemistry, molecules are groups of atoms bonded together, and take on new properties as a result.
  • In user interfaces, molecules are groups of elements that function together as a unit. For example, a form label, input field, and button can combine to form a search form molecule
  • Building up from atoms to molecules encourages a do one thing and do it well mentality ,and encourages the creation of reusable interface patterns.
