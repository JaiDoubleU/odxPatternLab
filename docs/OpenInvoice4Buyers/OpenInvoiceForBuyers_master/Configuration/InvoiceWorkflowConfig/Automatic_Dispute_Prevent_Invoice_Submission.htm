Automatic Dispute / Prevent Invoice Submission

OpenInvoice allows you to configure the business rules and data validation requirements that defines a valid invoice you are willing to accept from your suppliers. Based on your configuration, OpenInvoice will prevent your indexers (Image OBO) and online suppliers (Direct Entry, Image Scan, and LIDO) from submitting invoices that are not valid. In addition, OpenInvoice will automatically dispute on your behalf invoices from your integrated suppliers (B2B) that are not valid.

Note:Some validation rules do not apply to B2B invoices.

You define your business rules and data validations using:

·Company Preferences

·Invoice Data Field settings

·Coding Validations

·Invoice Workflow Rules

For information on how to make specific fields required as well as the options for validating one field based on other data elements please refer to Invoice Content Configuration.

For information on configuring invoice workflow rules, refer to Invoice Workflow Rules.

Online Suppliers and Image OBO

Direct Entry, LIDO CSV, LIDO XML, Image OBO, and Image Scan invoices are prevented from submission if:

·The invoice is rejected based on your company’s invoice workflow rules.

·A value is missing for an invoice data field that is required on invoice submission.

·The supplier referenced a purchase order that is not in OpenInvoice (an external PO) even though they have a valid purchase order in OpenInvoice and you have not configured the supplier to allow external purchase order references.

·The price book on the invoice and the price book on the referenced purchase order do not match.

·The supplier referenced a purchase order that is associated with a price book, but the price book was not selected in the invoice header.

·(Direct entry, LIDO CSV, and LIDO XML only) Your company has enabled the Prevent Invoice Submission on RED PO Compliance Alerts company preference and the invoice has a red purchase order alert.

Integrated Suppliers and Image Post

B2B and Image Post invoices will be auto-disputed if:

Note:Image Post invoices will only be auto-disputed if you have configured Dispute Management rules. Refer to Dispute Management for more information.

·The invoice is rejected based on your company’s invoice workflow rules.

·The supplier referenced a purchase order that is not in OpenInvoice (an external PO) even though they have a valid purchase order in OpenInvoice and you have not configured the supplier to allow external purchase order references.

·Your company has configured the Require PO company preference and the invoice is missing a purchase order reference.

·Your company has configured the Require Explicit PO Line company preference and at least one line on the invoice is missing a purchase order line reference.

·Your company has not enabled the Ignore PO mismatch on B2B invoice import company preference and the supplier site listed on the invoice is not the same as the supplier site listed on the referenced purchase order.

·Your company has configured the Require Invoice Line Service Dates company preference and at least one invoice line does not include the service dates.

·Your company has configured the Enable Units of Measure Compliance company preference and at least one invoice line has a value that is not in the defined list.
