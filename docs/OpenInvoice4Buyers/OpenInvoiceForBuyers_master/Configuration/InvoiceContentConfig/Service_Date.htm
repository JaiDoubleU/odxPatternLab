Service Date

The service date fields are used to enter the date that the work was completed. All invoices (detail or image) have a single service date available in the invoice header. Detail invoices, depending on your configuration, also have one or two service dates available on the invoice line.

Relevant Company Preferences

·Require Invoice Line Service Dates

Note:Detail invoices submitted from B2B suppliers will be disputed automatically on your company’s behalf if one of the include lines does not include a service date.

Makes the service date on the invoice header required for image invoices.

How to Make the Invoice Header Service Date Required

Note:The invoice header service date is always optional for detail invoices.

These procedures require the Corporate Admin permissions.

Enable the Require Invoice Line Service Dates Field Preference

Steps:

  1. Select Corporate > Company Preferences.

  2. Click Require Invoice Line Service Dates under the Invoice Content section.

  3. Select the Active checkbox.

  4. Click Submit.

How to Make the Invoice Line Service Date Required

Steps:

  1. Select Corporate > Company Preferences.

  2. Click Require Invoice Line Service Dates under the Invoice Content section.

  3. Select the Active checkbox.

  4. Click Submit.

  5. Select Corporate > Workflow Configuration.

  6. Click Invoice Data Fields.

  7. Select the Line Item Detail tab.

  8. Click Service Date.

  9. Select the appropriate workflow in the Required drop-down list.

  10. Click Submit.

How to Configure Supplier Access

Note:Suppliers are always able to view and modify the invoice header service date.

This procedure configures whether a supplier can view or modify Service Dates on a line item on an invoice.

Steps:

  1. Select Corporate > Workflow Configuration.

  2. Click Invoice Data Fields.

  3. Click the Line Item Detail tab.

  4. Click Service Date.

  5. Select the appropriate Supplier Access option from the drop-down list.

  6. Click Submit.

How to Configure Buyer Access

·Users in your company are always able to view the invoice header service date.Users in your company with the Invoice Modify IM permission are always able to modify the invoice header service date on image invoices submitted on behalf of the supplier.

This procedure configures whether users within your company are able to view or modify Service Dates on a line item on an invoice.

  1. Select Corporate > Workflow Configuration.

  2. Click Invoice Data Fields.

  3. Click the Line Item Detail tab.

  4. Click Service Date.

  5. Select the appropriate Buyer Access option from the drop-down list.

    6. Note:The buyer access Modify option is not supported for invoice line service dates.

  6. Click Submit.

Number of Date Fields

Note:The invoice header always has a single service date field.

Configure Data Type for Service Date

Steps:

  1. Select Corporate > Workflow Configuration.

  2. Click Invoice Data Fields.

  3. Click the Line Item Detail tab.

  4. Click Service Date.

  5. Select the appropriate Data Type from the drop-down list.

  6. Click Submit.
