Units of Measure

Units of measure are codes used to enforce compliance between the units entered on an invoice and other documents such as purchase orders and price books. The codes are case-sensitive.

Note:When manually creating an invoice, suppliers must select a unit of measure from your predefined list. Invoices submitted from B2B suppliers will be disputed automatically if one of the invoice lines does not include a valid unit of measure value.

How to Make Units of Measure a Required Field

These procedures require the Corporate Admin permissions.

Enable Units of Measure Compliance

Steps:

  1. Select Corporate > Company Preferences.

  2. Click Enable Units of Measure Compliance under the General section.

  3. Select the Active checkbox.

  4. Click Submit.

How to Add a Units of Measure Code

Steps:

  1. Select Corporate > Reference Data.

  2. Click Units of Measure.

  3. Click Add Record.

    4.  

  4. Enter the Unit of Measure information.

  5. Click Submit.
