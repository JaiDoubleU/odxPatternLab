Configuration Requirements

The following steps must be completed for your company to process payments through OpenInvoice. For detailed instructions on your company’s configuration, refer to “Configuring Payment Accounts” on page 11.

  1. Contact OpenInvoice Support to purchase the Payment license. Complete and sign the Client Account Agreement, which defines the payment financing method. If applicable, the agreement authorizes OpenInvoice’s money movement partner to withdraw funds from your designated bank account.

  2. In OpenInvoice, assign the Payment license to the appropriate users.

  3. Assign the appropriate permissions to view and use the OpenInvoice payment module.

  4. Configure your company’s payment accounts and payment terms.

  5. Define a group of people to receive notification of configuration errors and upcoming payments.

Your suppliers must be configured to receive payments through OpenInvoice. Your electronic suppliers (those that access OpenInvoice) will do their own configuration; however, you must configure your paper suppliers (those that do not access OpenInvoice) on their behalf. Complete the following configuration steps for each paper supplier site that will receive payments through OpenInvoice. For detailed instructions on configuring your paper invoice suppliers refer to “Configuring Unregistered Suppliers” on page 13.

  1. Enter the Pay-To Name, Remit To address, and Remit To contact information.

  2. Select the payment method (electronic or check).

    3. Note:The default is for the supplier to be paid by check. If the supplier wishes to be paid electronically, you must enter the appropriate banking information.

  3. Validate the information for payments.
