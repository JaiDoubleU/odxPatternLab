Unapprove Field Tickets

Buyers can revoke approval from, or unapprove, field tickets under the following conditions:

·Any user who can approve a ticket has permission to unapprove a ticket.

·The ticket has not been invoiced by the supplier.

·If the ticket has been invoiced, the invoice has been deleted or cancelled.

How to Unapprove a Field Ticket

Steps:

  1. Click Ticket > Ticket Application.

  2. Enter Field Ticket Number in the Search field, then click the Search icon.

  3. Click the ticket.

  4. Click the Unapprove button.

    5.  

  5. Enter a Comment explaining why the ticket is being unapproved.

  6. Click Yes, unapprove ticket. The ticket will be returned to the Submitted state, pending approval. The Ticket Journal will be updated to reflect the Unapprove action.

 

How to Bulk Approve Submitted Field Tickets

Buyers can bulk approve submitted tickets from suppliers.

Permission:Field Ticket Modify or Field Ticket Modify (restricted)

Note:The Field Ticket Modify (restricted) permission only allows you to modify tickets owned by you in a department where this permission is assigned. You own a ticket in a department either directly (when you are specified in the “owner” field) or indirectly (when the department name is specified in the “owner” field).

Note:Enable Bulk Approvals company preference must be selected.

Steps:

  1. Click Ticket > Ticket Application.

  2. Click a Ticket Stack.

  3. Select the checkbox next to the field tickets you want to approve.

  4. Click Approve. The bulk Approve Ticket window displays, which includes the number of tickets to be approved.

    5.  

  5. (Optional) Enter a comment.

  6. Click Yes, approve ticket.

Note:If any tickets cannot be approved, due to missing data required for approval, they will not be approved.
