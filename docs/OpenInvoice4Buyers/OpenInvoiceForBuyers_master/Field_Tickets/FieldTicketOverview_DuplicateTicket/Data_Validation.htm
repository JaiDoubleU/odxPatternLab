Data Validation

The following field ticket data is validated:

·AFE and Cost Center must be active values defined in OpenInvoice

·GL codes must be active, mapped combinations of Major / Minor / Subcode

·Requisitioner is the name provided by the buyer

·Service Dates must be between 10 years in the past and 1 year in the future

·Currency is either the last currency you selected or your default currency.

·Lines require part # / item ID, description, quantity, and unit of measure

·If configured, the unit of measure is validated against buyer list

For water hauling tickets:

·Driver and Truck # are required

·Hours Worked must be a positive integer between 0 and 23

·Minutes Worked must be a positive integer between 0 and 59

·The Feet and Inches on Gauge Start and Gauge Finish must be positive integers

·Volume must be an integer

For labor, equipment, material tickets:

·Charge Type is required
