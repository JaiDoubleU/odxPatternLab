Disputed Field Tickets

Much like an invoice, a field ticket can be disputed back to the supplier if there is something the buyer disagrees with.

How to Dispute a Field Ticket

Use this procedure to dispute a field ticket.

Steps:

  1. Enter Ticket Number in the Search field, then click the Search icon.

  2. Click the field ticket.

    3.  

  3. Click the Dispute button. The Confirm Dispute window appears.

    4.  

  4. Enter the Dispute Reason. Enter the reason the field ticket is being disputed. Include as much detail as necessary for the supplier to correct the problem and resubmit the field ticket for approval.

  5. Click Yes, dispute ticket.

You can view disputed field tickets while in the dispute state.

Note:Coding changes are automatically saved to the field ticket when the user navigates away from the field ticket.
