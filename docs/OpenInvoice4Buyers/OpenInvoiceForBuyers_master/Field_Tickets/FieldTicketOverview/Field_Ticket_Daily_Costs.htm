Field Ticket Daily Costs

The Daily Costs feature allows buyers to review all field tickets submitted to a specific AFE in a calendar format. When you select an AFE the page will refresh with a list of suppliers and costs by date. Selecting a cost total will provide a list of the associated field tickets.

How to View Daily Costs by AFE

Steps:

  1. Select Ticket > Ticket Application to open the Field Ticket application.

  2. Click the Menu button, then click Daily Costs.

  3. Start typing the AFE to display the list of AFEs, then select the one you want to view.

    4.  

    Note:The costs associated with the most current service dates are displayed.

  4. Click the daily cost amount to view the associated field tickets.
