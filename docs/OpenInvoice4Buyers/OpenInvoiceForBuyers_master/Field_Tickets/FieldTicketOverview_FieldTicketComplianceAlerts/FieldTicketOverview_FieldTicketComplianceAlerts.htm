Compliance of a Field Ticket Reference on an Invoice

Field Ticket compliance alerts notify buyers and suppliers when an invoice is not in compliance with the ticket references. The alerts are categorized by severity—red, yellow or green—with red being the most severe and green being the least severe. An invoice that is compliant with the referenced tickets will have a green alert.

.

Alert Name

Alert

Description

Line Total Mismatch

 

An invoice line references a FT line, where the total is different than the invoice line total after discount, with the tolerance of +/- one cent multiplied by the quantity. The tolerance accounts for rounding differences.

Note:Absence of rate on a FT line (and, therefore, absence of total) will not trigger this alert.

Ticket in Disputed Status

 

An invoice line references a disputed FT or its line.

Currency Mismatch

 

An invoice line references a FT line or an FT, where the currency is different than the invoice currency.

Note:Currencies must be truly different. Absence of currency on a FT line will not trigger this alert.

Product/Service Mismatch

 

An invoice line references a FT line with a product/service, where the product/service is different than the invoice line product/service.

Note:The product/service must be truly different - both invoice line and FT line have product/service.

Quantity Mismatch

 

An invoice line references a FT line, where the quantity is different than the invoice line quantity.

Note:Absence of quantity on a FT line will not trigger this alert.

The quantity may be positive or negative - does not matter.

Rate Mismatch

 

An invoice line references a FT line, where the rate is different than the invoice line rate after the discount with the tolerance of +/- one cent to account for rounding differences.

Note:Absence of a rate on a FT line will not trigger this alert.

Unit of Measure Mismatch

 

An invoice line references a FT line, where the unit of measure is different than the invoice line unit of measure.

Note:Absence of UoM on a FT line will not trigger this alert.

Ticket and/or Ticket Line Invoiced Multiple Times

 

Ticket and/or ticket line is referenced on multiple invoices.

Duplicate Ticket

 

A “new” FT referenced on an invoice line has the same number as a “new,” free-text or OIR FT specified on any other existing, non-cancelled, non-credit invoice line.

A free-text or OIR FT referenced on an invoice line has the same number as a “new” FT specified on any other existing, non-cancelled, non-credit invoice line.

Ticket in Submitted Status

 

An invoice line references a submitted (non-approved) FT or its line.

No Product/Service

 

An invoice line with a specific product/service references a FT line with no product/service or vice versa.

Ticket Reference Missing

 

An invoice line does not reference a FT line, while there is another line on this invoice referencing a FT or a FT line (not just free-text or OIR FT).

Ticket Uses Default Product/Service

 

Default product/service is used on a ticket.

No Ticket Rate

 

An invoice line references a FT line, which has no rate.

Service Date Mismatch

 

An invoice line references a FT line with a ‘service date from’ or ‘service date to’ different than the invoice line ‘service date from’ or ‘service date to’ or if an invoice line does not have service dates.

Coding Mismatch

 

An invoice line references a FT line with an AFE or CC or major or minor or subcode different than the invoice line coding, or if an invoice line does not have coding, but the ticket does have it.

No Ticket Quantity

 

An invoice line references a FT line, which has no quantity.

No Ticket Unit of Measure

 

An invoice line with a unit of measure references a FT line, which has no unit of measure or vice versa.

Ticket Compliant

 

An invoice line references a FT line and no amber or red TK alert conditions are triggered for this line.
