Payment Dashboard

The Payment Dashboard is accessed by going to Payment > Dashboard and consists of two portlets:

·The Payment Alerts portlet displays payments requiring supplier setup, payments requiring attention, and available credit.

·The Upcoming Payments portlet summarizes the next 15 scheduled payment impounds (starting on the current date).

 

Payment Alerts Portlet

This portlet alerts you to suppliers who require configuration for payments, payments requiring attention, and credits available to be applied against payments.

·Requiring Supplier Setup

§Not Configured for Payments: the number of enabled supplier sites that have not been configured to receive payments through OpenInvoice.

§Incorrect Configuration: the number of enabled supplier sites that require attention due to configuration errors.

·Requiring Attention

§Held: the number and total value of payments that have been put on hold. Click the value for the list of corresponding payments.

§Cancelled: the number and total value of payments that have been cancelled. Click the value for the list of corresponding payments.

§Void / Stopped: the number and total value of check payments that have been voided or stopped. This only applies to payments with a payment method of Check. Payments with a status of Check Stop Requested are not included in this count.

·Available Credit: the number and total remaining value of approved credit invoices. For example, if there is a single credit invoice of $1000 of which $750 has been applied to debit invoices the value displayed is $250.

Upcoming Payments Portlet

The Upcoming Payments portlet is sorted by financing date (impound date). The payment amount reflects the total amount to be transferred from your bank account to OpenInvoice on each financing date.

·Click Show All to see the expanded view that includes all scheduled payment impounds.

·Click a counter in the Payments column to view a list of payments included in the scheduled payment impound. The resulting list will be grouped by supplier. You can expand the supplier grouping to view individual payment details, including information from the invoice.
