Payment Overview

Buyers are able to process and manage payments using OpenInvoice. Once a user within your company approves an invoice for payment, OpenInvoice will generate and schedule a payment based on your company’s preferences and the invoice’s Early Pay discount eligibility. Suppliers have the option to receive payments processed by OpenInvoice either electronically (using ACH) or by check. If the supplier chooses to receive electronic payments, OpenInvoice’s money movement partner will transfer the funds directly into their bank account. If they choose to receive checks, OpenInvoice’s money movement partner will print the checks on your behalf and mail them to the supplier’s Remit To address.
