Payment Notifications

Payment notifications can be configured to be sent to role members.

Notifications Sent to Notification Role Members

If a payment account has been assigned a payment notification role, all members of the role will receive the following notifications.

·Seven Day Advance Notification: lists all payments that will be made in seven days. Users will receive one notification for all payments occurring on the same date. A notification will be sent even if there are no payments scheduled for the financing date.

Note:You configure the time of day that the notification will be sent on the Notifications tab of each Payment Account. If the time is not set then the notification will not be sent.

If you are configured for same day financing, any payments financed on the day they are generated/released will not be included in the notification.

·One Day Advance Notification: lists all payments that will be made in one day. Users will receive one notification for all payments occurring on the same date. A notification will be sent even if there are no payments scheduled for the financing date.

Note:You configure the time of day that the notification will be sent on the Notifications tab of each Payment Account. If the time is not set then the notification will not be sent.

If you are configured for same day financing, any payments financed on the day they are generated/released will not be included in the notification.

·Configuration (master data) failure notification: the supplier site is incorrectly configured to receive payments. The site’s address and/or bank details are incorrect.

·Returned item notification: an ACH payment has been returned because money cannot be transferred. The supplier site’s bank details are incorrect.
