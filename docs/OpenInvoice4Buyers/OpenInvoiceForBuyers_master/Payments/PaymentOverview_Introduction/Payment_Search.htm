Payment Search

Perform a payment search by specifying multiple search criteria. From the resulting list, you can request a report and perform actions on a batch of payments.

Note:For instructions on how to access your completed report, refer to the “Getting Started” section of the OpenInvoice User Guide for Buyers.

For instructions on how to perform actions on a batch of payments, refer to “Payment Workflow Actions” on page 24.

To search for paid invoices, refer to the “Invoice Overview” section of the OpenInvoice User Guide for Buyers.

How to Search for Payments

Permission:Payment View

Steps:

  1. Go to Payment > Payment Search.

    2.  

  2. Enter your search criteria.

    3. Note:If criteria is unknown, leave all fields blank to return a complete list. An asterisk can also be used to denote the unknown part of a name or number.

     

  3. Click Apply Filter.

Note:By default, payments are sorted by Supplier and Invoice Number. Sort by clicking any column header.
