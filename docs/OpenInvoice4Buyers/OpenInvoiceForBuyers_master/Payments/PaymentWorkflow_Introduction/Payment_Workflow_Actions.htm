Payment Workflow Actions

Users from your company can perform the following workflow actions in OpenInvoice.

·Manually manage credits by applying them to scheduled payments or marking them as having been settled externally

Note:OpenInvoice can be configured to automatically apply credits to scheduled payments. Refer to “Configuring Payment Terms” on page 15.

·Reschedule payments

·Place payments on hold

·Release held payments

·Terminate held payments, cancelled payments, or payments that have voided or stopped checks.

·Resubmit cancelled payments or payments that have voided or stopped checks.

Credits can only be applied to one payment in a single action however, if a credit is larger than the payment the remainder can be applied to another payment. Payments can be rescheduled, held, released, resubmitted, or terminated one at a time or as a batch.

Your company can request OpenInvoice Support to perform the following.

·Cancel posted payments that have not yet been sent to the bank for processing or the check has not been printed.

·Void checks that have been issued (printed) but not mailed

·Request a stop payment on checks that have been mailed but not cashed

Note:The payment will only be stopped if the bank is able to successfully execute the instructions before the check is cashed. Neither the bank nor OpenInvoice Support can guarantee that the payment will be stopped.

How to Manually Manage Credits

Permission:Payment Modify

Prerequisite:The payment is in Scheduled, Rescheduled, Held, Released, or Resubmitted status.

Note:Credits can only be applied to one payment at a time.

Steps:

  1. Do one of the following:

    2. §Go to Payment > Dashboard and click the Available Credit amount.

    §Go to Payment > Credits.

  2. Click a supplier site.

  3. Do one of the following:

§To apply a credit to a payment:

  1. Select the credit you want to apply.

  2. Select the payment you want the apply the credit against.

  3. Click Apply Credit.

  4. Repeat steps 1 through 3 if you want to apply the credit to more than one payment, or if you want to apply multiple credits to a payment.

§To remove a credit that has been settled externally:

  1. Select the credit.

  2. Click Settled Externally.

How to Place a Hold on One or More Payments

Permission:Payment Modify

Prerequisite:The payment is in Scheduled, Rescheduled, Released, or Resubmitted status.

Note:OpenInvoice will automatically hold a payment if a user from your company disables payment configuration or if the supplier site’s address is no longer verified for payments.

Steps:

  1. Do one of the following:

    2. §Go to Payment > Dashboard and click a counter in the Upcoming Payments portlet. If required, expand the supplier row to view multiple payments.

    §Perform a search with multiple criteria by going to Payment > Payment Search.

  2. Do one of the following:

    3. §To place a hold on a single payment, click the Payment Total.

    §To place a hold on a batch of payments, select the payments you want to hold.

  3. Click Hold.

  4. (Optional) Enter a comment.

  5. Click Submit.

How to Release One or More Held Payments

Permission:Payment Modify

Note:Payments that are automatically held by OpenInvoice must be manually released.

Steps:

  1. Do one of the following:

    2. §Go to Payment > Dashboard and click a counter in the Upcoming Payments portlet. If required, expand the supplier row to view multiple payments.

    §Perform a search with multiple criteria by going to Payment > Payment Search.

  2. Do one of the following:

    3. §To release a single help payment, click on the Payment Total.

    §To release a batch of held payments, select the payments you want to release.

  3. Click Release.

  4. (Optional) Enter a comment.

  5. Click Submit.

How to Terminate One or More Payments

Permission:Payment Modify

Prerequisite:The payment is in Pending, Held, Cancelled, Check Voided, or Check Stopped status.

Note:Terminated payments are removed from workflow. The associated invoice can be returned to workflow, adjusted, and once approved OpenInvoice will generate a new payment.

Steps:

  1. Do one of the following:

    2. §Go to Payment > Dashboard and click a counter in the Upcoming Payments portlet. If required, expand the supplier row to view multiple payments.

    §Perform a search with multiple criteria by going to Payment > Payment Search.

  2. Do one of the following:

    3. §To terminate a single payment, click the Payment Total.

    §To terminal a batch of payments, select the payments you want to terminate.

  3. Click Terminate.

  4. (Optional) Enter a comment.

  5. Click Submit.

    §Perform a search with multiple criteria by going to Payment > Payment Search.

  2. Do one of the following:

    3. §To resubmit a single payment, click the Payment Total.

    §To resubmit a batch of payments, select the payments you want to resubmit.

  3. Click Re-Submit.

  4. (Optional) Enter a comment.

  5. Click Submit.
