Payment Status

This section contains the available payment status attributes within the OpenInvoice platform.

      

Status

Description

Scheduled

OpenInvoice has scheduled the payment according to the defined payment terms and your company has not made any adjustments to the payment date. Payment details have not been sent to OpenInvoice’s money movement partner for processing.

Rescheduled

A user from your company has manually adjusted the payment date. Payment details have not been sent to OpenInvoice’s money movement partner for processing.

Held

A condition has occurred that prevents the payment from being processed or a user from your company has manually removed the payment from workflow.

Released

A user from your company has manually returned the payment to workflow.

Posted

Payment details have been sent to OpenInvoice’s money movement partner for processing, but the transaction has not yet completed.

Cancelled

A user from your company has requested that payment to the supplier be stopped, but the payment has already been posted.

Paid by ACH

OpenInvoice’s money movement partner has completed the payment transaction and funds have been transferred to the supplier via ACH.

Check Issued

OpenInvoice’s money movement partner has completed the payment transaction and a check has been issued for payment. The supplier has not yet cashed the check and your company has not requested a check stop.

Check Voided

A user from your company has requested that the payment be stopped before the check was mailed or the check was returned by the post office as undeliverable.

Check Stop Requested

A user from your company has requested that the payment be stopped after the check was mailed. A stop payment request is sent to the bank, but the stop is not guaranteed.

Check Stop Denied

The stop payment request was unsuccessful, allowing the supplier to cash the check at the bank.

Check Stopped

The stop payment request was successful and the check cannot be cashed by the supplier at the bank.

Check Settled

The supplier has cashed the check and funds have been transferred.

Returned

OpenInvoice’s money movement partner was unable to complete the payment transaction due to a configuration error.

Resubmitted

A user from your company has restarted the payment process for an invoice with a previously cancelled or stopped payment.

Terminated

The payment is marked as end-of-life and the invoice will not be paid through OpenInvoice.

Note:The associated invoice can be returned to workflow, adjusted, and once approved OpenInvoice will generate a new payment.

Credit Applied

The original payment amount has been reduced by applying a credit. The remaining amount due is reflected in the payment amount and the corresponding funds will be transferred on the scheduled dates.

Paid by Applying Credit

The entire payment amount has been paid by applying a credit. No funds will be transferred and no further processing is required.

Payment Not Required

Payment is for an invoice with zero value ($0.00). No further processing is required.
